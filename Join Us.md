## Opportunities

We welcome motivated undergraduate, master's, and Ph.D. students who are interested in semiconductor physics, nanotechnology, and advanced materials to join our research team.
## What You Will Learn

In our lab, you will gain hands-on training in semiconductor fabrication and experimental techniques used in modern device research. You will learn how to turn creative ideas into functional engineering solutions, build strong data analysis skills, and uncover the physical mechanisms that govern device behavior. We also emphasize the ability to communicate science clearly through effective presentations and scientific storytelling. Students are encouraged to integrate artificial intelligence tools into their workflow, enabling smarter data analysis, faster research iteration, and more innovative project development.
